What Is AI?

05Definition

Artificial Intelligence

AI = computer systems performing tasks requiring human intelligence: learning, patterns, decisions, language, problem-solving.

06Clarify

AI Is and Is NOT

IS:

  • Pattern recognition
  • Statistical prediction
  • Cognitive automation
  • Tool augmenting humans
  • Only as good as data

Is NOT:

  • Conscious
  • Always correct
  • Replacing ALL jobs
  • Movie robots
  • Magic

11Supervised

How It Works

10,000 labeled examples → learns patterns → classifies NEW data.

Spam: Train on labeled emails → finds word/sender patterns → auto-classifies new mail

12Unsupervised

How It Works

Data without labels → AI discovers hidden groups on its own.

Customers: 100K histories → AI finds 5 buyer types nobody knew existed

21GIGO

Garbage In, Garbage Out

AI only as good as its data. Poor data = wrong predictions, biased outcomes.

ProblemImpact
InaccurateWrong predictions
IncompleteBiased model
Not representativeUnfair outcomes

22Bias

Real AI Bias Cases

  • Amazon hiring (2018) – penalized women
  • Facial recognition – higher error darker skin
