Automation Risk Formula

A job is automatable if it is:

✓ Repetitive (same task over and over)
✓ Rule-based (clear if-then logic)
✓ Predictable (stable environment)
✓ Data-heavy (involves processing lots of structured data)

A job is SAFE if it requires:
✗ Creativity and original thinking
✗ Complex social interaction / empathy
✗ Physical dexterity in unpredictable environments
✗ Strategic judgment with incomplete information

The ATM Paradox

When ATMs were introduced in the 1970s, everyone predicted bank tellers would disappear. What actually happened? The number of bank tellers INCREASED. Why? ATMs made branches cheaper to operate, so banks opened MORE branches. Tellers shifted from counting cash to advising customers on loans, investments, and services. The job TRANSFORMED, not disappeared.

The Human + Machine Formula

The future isn't Human VS Machine.
It's Human + Machine > Either alone.

The radiologist who uses AI to screen X-rays is better than either the radiologist alone OR the AI alone. AI catches patterns humans miss; humans catch context AI misses.

T-Shaped Professional

Deep Expertise + Broad Knowledge

The "T" shape: deep expertise in ONE area (the vertical bar) + broad knowledge across many areas (the horizontal bar). Example: deep in IS management + broad understanding of AI, data, security, cloud, UX. This makes you adaptable and hard to replace.

BSIS: Your Automation Advantage

  • You understand BOTH business AND technology (rare combination)
  • You can evaluate WHERE automation makes sense (not everywhere)
  • You can MANAGE the human side of automation (change management)
  • You design the SYSTEMS that integrate humans and machines
  • You're learning NOW while others will scramble later

Ethical Automation Framework

  • 1. Automate tasks, not entire livelihoods (augment workers)
  • 2. Provide transition support (retraining, severance, timeline)
  • 3. Share productivity gains (don't keep ALL savings as profit)
  • 4. Maintain human oversight for critical decisions
  • 5. Consider social impact, not just business ROI
