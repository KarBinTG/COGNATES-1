⏸️ Practice Short Answer

Write 2 sentences:

"Explain privacy by design and give one example for a student information system."

Can you answer WITHOUT notes? Try it now.

⏱️ 2 min writing, then model answer

Model Answer Example

Full-marks answer:

"Privacy by design means building privacy protection INTO the system from the START, not adding it as an afterthought (proactive, not reactive). For a student IS: encrypt all personal data by default, collect ONLY necessary information (data minimization), and allow students to view/delete their own records without requesting approval."

✅ Gets Full Marks

Definition + principle + specific applied example

❌ Gets Zero

"Privacy is important and we should protect it." (Vague, no application)
